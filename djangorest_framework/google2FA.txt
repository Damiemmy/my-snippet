# 🔐 Enable Google 2-Step Verification (2FA) — Step-by-Step Guide

## 🧭 STEP 0 — Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure:

* You are signed into your Google account
* You have access to your phone number or Authenticator app

---

## 🪜 STEP 1 — Open Google Security Settings

1. Open your browser
2. Go to: [https://myaccount.google.com/security](https://myaccount.google.com/security)
3. Scroll to **“How you sign in to Google”**

---

## 🪜 STEP 2 — Locate 2-Step Verification

1. Find **2-Step Verification**
2. Click on it

---

## 🪜 STEP 3 — Start Setup

1. Click **Get Started**
2. Re-enter your Google password when prompted

---

## 🪜 STEP 4 — Choose Verification Method

Google will ask how you want to verify your identity.

Choose one:

* 📱 Google Prompt (recommended)
* 📲 SMS (phone number)
* 🔐 Google Authenticator App

👉 Recommended for beginners: **SMS**

---

## 🪜 STEP 5 — Verify Phone Number

1. Enter your phone number
2. Google sends a 6-digit code
3. Enter the code

---

## 🪜 STEP 6 — Enable 2FA

1. Click **Turn On**
2. 2-Step Verification will be activated

---

## 🪜 STEP 7 — Confirm Activation

You should see:

* ✅ 2-Step Verification = ON

---

# 🔑 AFTER 2FA (IMPORTANT FOR DEVELOPERS)

## 🪜 STEP 8 — Generate App Password (for Django SMTP)

1. Go to: [https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords](https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords)
2. Select:

   * App: Mail
   * Device: Other (e.g. Django Server)
3. Click **Generate**
4. Copy the 16-digit password

---

## ⚠️ KEY RULES

* ❌ Never use your real Gmail password for apps
* ✅ Use App Password for Django, SMTP, etc.
* ✅ 2FA must be enabled before App Passwords work

---

# 🧠 ALGORITHM SUMMARY

```
START
 → Open Google Security Page
 → Navigate to 2-Step Verification
 → Click Get Started
 → Verify Password
 → Choose SMS / Authenticator
 → Verify Code
 → Enable 2FA
 → Confirm status = ON
 → Generate App Password (optional)
END
```
