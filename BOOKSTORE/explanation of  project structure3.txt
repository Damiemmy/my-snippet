1.)Why We Created apps/: business domain
You saw:
    - backend/apps/
        users/
        books/
        cart/
        orders/

Why?
Because apps represent business domains.
Imagine Amazon.


backend/ : 
    config/
    apps/
    core/

These are three different responsibilities:

config:- brain:
    - settings
    - urls
    - wsgi
    - asgi
    - celery


app-Business:
    - users/
    - books/
    - cart/
    - orders/

core-shared tools:
    - permissions
    - pagination
    - exceptions
    - middleware
